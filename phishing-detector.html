🚩 Red Flags Checklist

  • Misspelled or look-alike domains (paypa1.com, amaz0n.com)
  • Urgent language: "Act now!", "Your account will be suspended!"
  • Generic greetings: "Dear Customer" instead of your name
  • Suspicious links that don't match the claimed sender
  • Requests for passwords, credit cards, or personal info
  • Too-good-to-be-true offers or unexpected prizes
  • Attachments you didn't expect (invoices, documents)
  • Mismatched "From" address and display name
  • Poor grammar or unusual formatting
  • Pressure to act immediately or face consequences
  • Links with unusual TLDs or extra subdomains
  • Emails claiming to be from IT asking for credentials
