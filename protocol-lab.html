🏆 Verdict

  • Use WireGuard when: you want speed, battery efficiency, simplicity, mobile use
  • Use OpenVPN when: you need TCP for restrictive firewalls, maximum compatibility, older devices
  • Our recommendation: WireGuard for 95% of use cases. It's faster, uses less code, and reconnects instantly after sleep.
