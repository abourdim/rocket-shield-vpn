Attack techniques you learned

  • SSH brute force — attackers try thousands of passwords on port 22. Mitigate with key-auth, fail2ban.
  • DNS hijack — redirecting domain resolution to attacker IPs. Use DoH/DoT and DNSSEC.
  • Data exfiltration — unusual outbound traffic volumes at odd times is a red flag.
  • Port scan — a recon technique mapping open services. Detect with IDS (Snort, Suricata).
  • ARP spoofing / MITM — two MACs claiming one IP. Use static ARP or dynamic ARP inspection.
